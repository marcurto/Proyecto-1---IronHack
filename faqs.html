FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

¿WHAT IS YOUR DAY?

Your Day is an entertainment-type website that allows you to know which song was the most sounded the day you were born, which movie was the most viewed and which book was the best seller.

¿WHAT DOES YOUR DAY OFFERS YOU?

Apart from showing you these three elements, it also shows some of its curiosities and allows you to register and log in.

¿WHAT IS THE ORIGIN OF YOUR DAY DATA?

Your Day obtiene toda su información a través de diferentes APIs. Por ejemplo, utiliza una API de creación propia a partir de datos obtenidos de Billaboard, la API de Genius o la API de iMDB.
